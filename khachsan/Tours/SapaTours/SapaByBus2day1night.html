Sapa by bus 2 days 01 night - overnight in Hotel 3 star

This tour is designed for travelers who want to spend 2 days - 1 night in Sapa aiming at: Saving the time, visit the most popular travel sites in Sapa, stay overnight in a good hotel to be comfortable, much lower cost than going by night train, especially you can enjoy the friendly services provided by our team

This tour is designed for travelers who want to spend 2 days - 1 night in Sapa aiming at: Saving the time, visit the most popular travel sites in Sapa, stay overnight in a good hotel to be comfortable, much lower cost than going by night train, especially you can enjoy the friendly services provided by Amoritaboutiquehotel.com
Duration: 2 days 1 night
Destinations: Sapa, Cat Cat, Y Linh Ho, Lao Chai, Ta Van
Day 1: Hanoi – Sapa – Lao Chai – Ta Van (L/D)
06:30: Car and our guides pick you up at Hanoi Old Quarter and depart for Sapa. It takes 6 hours to arrive Sapa. During the journey, you will have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful scenery on the both sides of this road.
12:30: Arriving Sa Pa, our guide will picks you up at bus station and take you go to our hotel.
13:00: Visitors started trekking journey about 4 hours to visit Lao Chai – Ta Van. You have an opportunity to explore the life of ethnic minorities, admire the spectacular natural scenery with the terraced rice fields. Visiting Sapa ancient rock with engraved images rocks, unique patterns of the ancients; admire the Bridge which made by clouds, cross the Muong Hoa stream poetic are an interesting moment.
Our driver will take you get back to Sapa. You have break and enjoy dinner at the hotel.
In the evening, you have free time to discover Sapa town.
Overnight at the hotel.
Day 2: Cat Cat village (B/L)
You have breakfast at the hotel.
9:00 am: You start the trip on foot to explore the Cat Cat – Sin Chai village of ethnic Hmong – discovered by the French and choose resort here in the early twentieth century.
Coming to Cat Cat Village, visitors will be seen hydropower station, waterfall of the Cat Cat village and learned about the culture, customs and unique people here with traditional crafts such as weaving, touching carved silver, forged agricultural tools, to drag his wife…
12:00: Pick up from Cat Cat village back to Sapa town. Enjoy lunch at the hotel then you have free to visit Sapa town for shopping souvenirs and prepare to come back with a lot of beautiful and unforgettable memories.
15:00: The driver takes you go to bus station. You get the bus to come back Hanoi.
22:00: You arrive Ha Noi. End of tour.
