Incredibla asia journeys - Ha Long bay 1 day trip

Ho chi minh city tour and cu chi tunnels - 01 Day (start 8:00 AM return 5:00 PM)

Halong Bay is an attractive destination for most Vietnam Tours. A day cruise with Incredible Asia Journeys is the best choice for you to experience the magnificent Halong Bay with our ‘all-in-one’ itinerary for those with a limited time schedule. You will not only explore one of the most beautiful caves in Halong Bay but will also have the chance to go kayaking or bamboo boat rowing, as well as...

- 07h45 – 08h30 : Pick-up at hotel and start the scenic, 3.5-hour drive to Halong Bay. Enjoy the landscape of the Red River Delta countryside and have a short break on the way.
- 12h45 – 13h00 : You arrive the Tuan Chau Habor. Then embark the Incredible Asia Journeys – Day cruise followed by a short cruise briefing.
- 13h00 – 16h45 : Start a dreamy cruise to stunning scenery with thousands of limestone mountains and have seafood lunch (we serve you with Vietnamese traditional dishes, in case you are vegetarian, we have other food options for you) while cruising through the emerald water passing by the Dog Stone, the Burning Incense islet, stop at Ba Hang fishing village for kayaking or sit in a bamboo boat rowed by locals.
After the visiting of Fighting-Cock island and the Heavenly Palace (Thien Cung cave) – which has opened to the public since 1998 with stalagmites and stalactites. Back to the Incredible Asia Journeys – Day cruise and continue cruising back to the jetty.
- 16h45 : Back to Tourist wharf. Disembark and get on bus to Hanoi.
- 20h30 : Arrive in Hanoi. Our tour guide and driver say goodbye to you at your hotel. Trip ends.
