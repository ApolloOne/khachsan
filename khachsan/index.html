About Us

Rooms at the Amorita Boutique are fitted with hardwood furniture. Each room is equipped with a minibar and refrigerator. En suite bathrooms have hot shower facilities.The hotel provides currency exchange and car rental services. A tour desk can help with travel and sightseeing arrangements.Guests can have their meals at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Vietnamese dishes.Hoan Kiem is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, street food and city walks.This is our guests' favorite part of Hanoi, according to independent reviews.
We speak your language! Amorita Boutique Hotel has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 18 April, 2017
